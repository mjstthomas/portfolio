About Me

Hello, I'm Matt.
A full stack web developer in training.


I am currently working through the software engineering program at Thinkful. I started my journey into software engineering soon after the birth of my twin boys. I took on the mentality of, "When is the last time you did something for the first time?" Which has lead me to trying something I have always been too afraid to do. It started with a fun etch-a-sketch app that my daughter loved. Then, a To Do list with transitioning properties. Turns out, I love it. You can find me in my free time playing on code wars or looking up the newest CSS tricks.


Being a retail manager, I believe leadership and teamwork are what drives success. I believe in the mantra of "I fail and We succeed" and that everyone needs to work together for a specific goal or we will never reach it. As I have worked more with software programming, I began to see how I could take my 16 years of food service retail experience, working with a team of like-minded developers, we could come up with truly useful applications that will drive the future of the industry.
