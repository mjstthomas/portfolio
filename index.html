I am currently working through the software engineering program at Thinkful. I started my journey into software engineering soon after the birth of my twin boys. I took on the mentality of, "When is the last time you did something for the first time?", which has lead me to trying something I have always been too afraid to do. It started with a fun etch-a-sketch app that my daughter loved. Then, a To Do list with transitioning properties. Turns out, I love it.


In my free time, if you can call it that, I lead a pretty full life. My 5 children and wife always keep me on my toes with adventures to go on. From hiking to just playing on the trampoline out back. They have definitely helped me to get out and train for the Worlds Toughest Mudder that I would like to do this next year. In my alone time though, you can find me playing on code wars or looking up the newest CSS tricks.


Being a retail manager, I believe leadership and teamwork are what drives success. I believe in the mantra of "I fail and We succeed", and that everyone needs to work together for a specific goal or we will never reach it. As I have worked more with software programming, I began to see how I could take my 16 years of food service retail experience, working with a team of like-minded developers, we could come up with truly useful applications that will drive the future of the industry.
